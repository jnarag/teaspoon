## Citation

If you use this software, *please* cite us - this helps us maintain and improve TEASPOON.

To cite the TEASPOON software itself, use:

- Raghwani et al. (2018 in prep.)

### Method citation

To cite the method, use:

- Raghwani et al. (2016)
- Bhatt et al. (2011)
